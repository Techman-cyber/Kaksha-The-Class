What is Kaksha – The Class?

Kaksha – The Class is your friendly learning space for Hindi-B (CBSE classes 1 to 12). We provide clear explanations, PDF notes, Q&A for stories, grammar topics with video lessons, practice quizzes, and full-length tests. Learn in a fun, easy way!
